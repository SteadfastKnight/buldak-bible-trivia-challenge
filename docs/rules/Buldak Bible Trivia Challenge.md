# 🔥 Buldak Bible Trivia Challenge — Official Rules

## Overview
A spicy noodle eating challenge combined with Bible trivia. Survive the heat, answer questions, and outsmart your opponents — but no one knows who's winning until the very end.

---

## Setup
- **Players:** 5–8
- **Rounds:** 6–8 rounds
- **Noodle options:** Buldak Carbonara or Buldak 2x Spicy (player's choice)
- **Available relief:** Water and Milk (use at your own cost)
- **Scores:** Hidden until the end. After Round 4, rankings only (1st, 2nd, 3rd...) are announced — not actual scores.

---

## Before the Game Starts
1. Players are given **2 minutes** to choose their noodle: **Carbonara** or **2x Spicy**.
2. Choices are locked in and announced. 2x Spicy players are marked with a 🔥 for the duration of the game.
3. Remind everyone: **no relief items until a round is in progress.**

---

## How Each Round Works
1. Everyone takes a **forkful of noodles** — as equal in size as possible — and eats simultaneously.
2. Once the spice kicks in, each player may **optionally** choose one trivia question:
   - 🟢 **Easy** — 100 points
   - 🟡 **Medium** — 250 points
   - 🔴 **Hard** — 500 points
3. Players may **skip** answering — no penalty for passing.
4. **Incorrect answers** are penalized (see Scoring Table).
5. Relief items may be used at any point during a round.
6. Players who have tapped out do not participate in that round.

---

## The 🔥 2x Spicy Multiplier

Players who chose 2x Spicy have a **3x multiplier** applied to ALL point changes — gains and losses — except bonuses, which are flat for everyone.

| | Carbonara | 2x Spicy (3x) |
|---|---|---|
| Easy correct ✅ | +100 pts | +300 pts |
| Medium correct ✅ | +250 pts | +750 pts |
| Hard correct ✅ | +500 pts | +1,500 pts |
| Easy wrong ❌ | −50 pts | −150 pts |
| Medium wrong ❌ | −100 pts | −300 pts |
| Hard wrong ❌ | −200 pts | −600 pts |
| Skip | 0 | 0 |
| Water 💧 | −75 pts | −225 pts |
| Milk 🥛 | −150 pts | −450 pts |

---

## Bonuses (Flat for All Players)
- 🏅 **Survival Bonus:** +50 pts per round completed without tapping out
- 🏆 **Last Standing Bonus:** +500 pts if you are the last active player remaining

---

## Relief Penalties
- 💧 **Water:** −75 pts (−225 for 2x Spicy)
- 🥛 **Milk:** −150 pts (−450 for 2x Spicy)

Each use is penalized separately. Players may use relief as many times as they want per round.

---

## Tapping Out
A player may tap out at any point. They **keep all points earned so far** but cannot earn any more. They remain on the scoreboard.

---

## Minimum Active Players
The game requires **at least 2 active players** to continue. If it drops to 1, that player receives the **Last Standing Bonus (+500 pts)** and the game ends immediately with a score reveal.

---

## Mid-Game Reveal (After Round 4)
Rankings only are announced (1st, 2nd, 3rd...) — no actual scores revealed.

---

## Ending the Game
All scores revealed simultaneously after the final round. Highest score wins.

> **Tiebreaker:** Tied players face one sudden-death Hard question. First correct answer wins.
